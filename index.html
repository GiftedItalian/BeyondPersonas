ABOUT THE PROJECT

Beyond Personas is a collection of non-fungible characters called "Personas" launched on the DigiByte blockchain and created by GiftIt. Each Persona has a unique style which varies from different genders, ethnicities, hair, body types, clothing, facial expressions, poses and more. A new Persona is created, minted and auctioned on DigiAssetX every 2 weeks. The aim of the project is to contribute to the DigiByte ecosystem, grow the Beyond Personas community and help bring people together.

HOW PERSONAS ARE MADE

DESIGNING

Every Persona in the collection is a different human-created 3D character. These detailed 3D characters are designed using Daz Studios software. The 3D software provides a library of realistic assets which allows a Persona to be created in a short amount of time.

RENDERING

Once a Persona has been designed within Daz Studios, these Personas are then exported and imported into Blender 3D modeling software. Tweaks are made to the model in Blender and then the Cycles Engine is used to render a realistic Persona for the collection.
